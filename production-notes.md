The *.source.png images in ./images are screenshots from the original source scans. These were processed in Pixelmator to be black and white with 100% contrast, then in some cases adjusted with a new layout to better fit the responsive nautre of ebooks, before being saved to the same directory. Finally, they were automatically traced in Inkscape (with a manually selected brightness cutoff for best results) before being shrunk with SVGO and having titles added, and then transferred to the epub/src/images directory.
